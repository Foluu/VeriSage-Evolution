CHURCH FINANCE REPORT

TREM Monthly Return of Receipts and Payments

Guidelines for Completing the Form

1. Kindly complete the form fields below with the appropriate data.
2. For fields that don't apply, leave them empty or input "0". Required fields are marked with '*'
3. Report MUST be submitted not later than the 15th day of the subsequent month
(e.g. Report for the month of June is expected not later than 15th of July)
4. Every Report submitted would be registered as 25th of the selected month in the batch file.
